Armani Dolci

A project realized while working for 150UP, a Milan based creative agency, during a six months internship. I provided services as 3D artist, modeler, animator, texture artist and in a few cases art director. Armani Dolci is a social advertising campaign by Armani for the Easter Holidays, Ramadam and Valentine's Day.
