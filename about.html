HI! I’m Giacomo Gambarana, I’m a 3D artist based in Milan.

I studied Game Art at SAE Institute. Here I developed both technical skills as well as a solid creative workflow, being able to approach and deliver on every 3D art related project with versatility, precision and quality.

From November 2022 to April 2023, I completed an internship at 150UP, a creative agency based in Milan. During this period I worked on advertising projects for brands like Armani, Alessi, Kartell, Driade and Fontana Arte.

I have a profound interest for cinema and for the horror genre in particular, which is a constant source of inspiration for my individual works.
